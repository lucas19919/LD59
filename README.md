# The Terraformer

https://ldjam.com/events/ludum-dare/59/$424615 

You are a **Lone Automated Terraforming Unit (LATU)** deployed to a barren exoplanet in a faraway solar system. Your mission: Terraform the wasteland.

![The Terraformer Gameplay](https://github.com/lucas19919/LD59/blob/main/main.png?raw=true)

---

## Overview

To function, you must maintain a **Signal** with your crew. Signal is supplied by already deployed Signal Towers on the exoplanet's surface. Place further Signal Towers, expand, and terraform the barren world into a lush, green environment.

## Mechanics

* **Signal:** Signal (dBm) is your currency. Charge it by staying close to Signal Towers and spend it to expand your reach.
* **Expansion:** Use your signal to build more Signal Towers to increase your operational range across the planet.
* **Terraforming:** Your primary goal is to plant and sustain **25 trees** to complete a successful terraform.
* **Care:** Trees must be watered to survive. A lack of water is indicated by shrinking and yellowing leaves.
