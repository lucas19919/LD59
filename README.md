# \# The Terraformer

# You are a Lone Automated Terraforming Unit, (LATU), deployed to a barren exoplanet in a faraway solar system. Your mission: Terraform the wasteland.

# 

# To function, ensure to maintain a Signal with your crew. Signal is supplied by already deployed Signal Towers on the exoplanet's surface. Place further Signal Towers, expand, and terraform the barren world into a green environment.

# \## Mechanics

# 

# \* Signal: Signal (/ dBm) is your currency. Charge it close to Signal Towers, spend it to expand.

# \* Expansion: Use your signal to build more signal towers to expand your range across the planet.

# \* Terraforming: Your goal is to plant and sustain 25 trees to complete a successful terraform.

# \* Care: To ensure the wellbeing of each tree, they must be watered. A lack of water is indicated through shrinking and yellow leaves.

#
